Welcome! Find guides, FAQs, and resources to help you get the most out of RequestFlow.

Core Features

Ticket Management

Streamline your support with our powerful ticketing system. Assign, track, and resolve customer issues efficiently with custom statuses, priorities, and SLA tracking.

Project Management

Organize your work into projects. Manage tasks, track budgets, set deadlines, and link tickets to projects for a complete overview of all related work.

Multi-Channel Support

Meet your customers where they are. Integrate with Email, WhatsApp, and embeddable Web Forms to create tickets from any channel automatically.

Onboarding Guide

Administrator Setup

As an Administrator, you have full control. Your first steps are:

  1. Sign Up: Create your organization account from the main signup page.
  2. Manage Users: Navigate to "User Accounts" to create accounts for your Agents and Clients.
  3. Configure Channels: Go to "Channels" to set up your support email, WhatsApp number, or get the code for your web form.
  4. Customize Settings: Explore the "Settings" page to personalize workflows, email templates, and access controls.

Agent Quickstart

As an Agent, your focus is on resolving tickets and contributing to projects.

  1. First Login: Use the credentials provided by your Administrator. Change your password immediately in "Settings" > "Account".
  2. Manage Tickets: The "Tickets" dashboard is your main workspace. View assigned tickets, reply to customers, and update ticket properties.
  3. Work on Projects: If assigned to projects, you can view them in the "Projects" section and manage tasks as per the permissions set by your manager.

Client Portal Guide

As a Client, you can easily submit and track your support requests.

  1. Submit Tickets: Go to the "Tickets" section and click "New Ticket" to describe your issue. You can also reply to an email from the support team to update your ticket.
  2. Track Progress: View a list of all your submitted tickets and their current status.
  3. Collaborate: Add replies and attachments to your tickets to provide more information to the support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password from the login page by clicking the 'Forgot Password?' link. If you are already logged in, you can change your password under Settings > Account.

How do I add a new user to my organization?

As an Administrator, you can add new users by navigating to the 'User Accounts' page and clicking the 'Create User' button. You'll need to provide their name, email, role, and an initial password.

Can clients create tickets from within the app?

Yes, clients can create tickets by navigating to the 'Tickets' page and clicking the 'New Ticket' button. They can also create tickets by sending an email to your configured support address or via WhatsApp.
